## Digital Advocacy & Community Building

In a connected world, the ability to communicate a message effectively is a vital entrepreneurial skill. Our social media program teaches students how to use digital platforms for advocacy, community outreach, and philanthropic awareness.

We move beyond simple social interactions to focus on impactful storytelling and data-driven engagement. By managing the Foundation's digital presence, learners gain hands-on experience in content strategy and community management, learning how to mobilize support for STEM education and bridge the gap between technology and the people who need it most.

Follow our accounts for the latest in STEM-related tech, news, and education!
