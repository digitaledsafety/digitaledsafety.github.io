## Building the Future with Open Source

<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-8 mb-3">
<p>
Programming is the core of modern innovation. Our programs focus on bridging the digital divide by teaching students how to build, maintain, and scale software. By leveraging open-source technologies, we provide a collaborative environment where learners can see their work impact the community directly.
</p>
<p>
Software development is more than just writing code; it's a tool for social impact. We empower our participants to move from being passive consumers of technology to becoming active creators and problem solvers.
</p>
<p>
The Digital Education & Safety Foundation developed a specialized fork of the Scratch 3.0 Visual Programming platform. This environment includes custom extensions designed to introduce students to the intersection of creative coding and digital entrepreneurship. It is available free for use <a href="https://scratch.digitaleducationsafety.org">here</a>.
</p>
