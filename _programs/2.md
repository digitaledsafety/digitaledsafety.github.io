## Digital Entrepreneurship & Web 3

<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-8 mb-3">
<p>
Web 3 represents a shift toward a more decentralized and creator-centric digital economy. Our brag.charity program introduces students to the fundamentals of blockchain, cryptocurrency, and digital assets. We focus on the practical application of these technologies for social good and personal empowerment.
</p>
<p>
Students learn not only the "how" of creating digital assets but also the "why"—understanding market dynamics, digital ownership, and the entrepreneurial mindset required to succeed in the modern economy. By integrating technical skills with marketing strategy, we prepare the next generation for the evolving digital marketplace.
</p>
<p>
Ultimately, this project encourages innovation and entrepreneurial thinking while bridging the gap between traditional education and the dynamic world of blockchain technology, ensuring that STEM education remains both relevant and engaging for future generations.
</p>
