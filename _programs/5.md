## The Business of STEM

Entrepreneurship is one of our core pillars. Our Ecommerce program provides a practical look at how digital businesses operate—from requirement gathering and inventory management to marketing and fulfillment.

By engaging with the foundation's store, students learn the technical side of e-commerce platforms and the business principles of sustainability. Every purchase supports our mission to provide free STEM education, making this a true example of how technology and business can work together for community empowerment.
