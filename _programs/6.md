## Navigating the AI Frontier

<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-8 mb-3">
<p>
Artificial Intelligence is reshaping how we learn and work. Our AI program focuses on AI literacy—ensuring that the under-served have access to the same cutting-edge tools as everyone else. We emphasize the responsible use of AI as a partner in the creative and educational process.
</p>
<p>
Our AI STEM Assistant is a prime example of this mission. Designed for educational safety and brevity, it helps learners navigate complex STEM topics across Science, Engineering, and the Arts. We empower our community to not just use AI, but to understand its potential to create a more inclusive and innovative future.
</p>
