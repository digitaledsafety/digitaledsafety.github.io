In the field of artificial intelligence (AI), a hallucination or artificial hallucination (also called confabulation or delusion) is a response by an AI which contains false or misleading information presented as fact. This phenomenon is particularly common in large language models, where the model generates text that is syntactically correct but factually incorrect.
