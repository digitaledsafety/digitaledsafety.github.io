Fine-tuning is the process of adapting a model trained for one task (the upstream task) to perform a different, usually more specific, task (the downstream task). It is considered a form of transfer learning, as it reuses knowledge learned from the original training objective. Fine-tuning involves applying additional training to the parameters of a neural network that have been pre-trained.
