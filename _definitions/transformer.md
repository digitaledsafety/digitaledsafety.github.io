A transformer is a deep learning architecture based on the multi-head attention mechanism. It is notable for its ability to handle sequential data, such as natural language, without requiring that the data be processed in a specific order. Transformers have become the dominant architecture for many natural language processing tasks, and are the basis for models like BERT and GPT.
