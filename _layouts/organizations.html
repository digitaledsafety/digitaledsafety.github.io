Empowering STEM Education

Our foundation is dedicated to supporting organizations that empower the next generation through modern STEM education. We believe that by providing access to quality resources, mentorship, and hands-on learning experiences, we can help bridge the gap for under-served communities and prepare students for the careers of tomorrow.

The organizations listed below share our commitment to fostering innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. From coding initiatives and robotics programs to digital safety and technology standards, these partners are at the forefront of educational transformation.

We encourage you to explore the work of each organization to learn more about their specific missions and the impact they are making in the STEM ecosystem. Your support, whether through donations, volunteering, or advocacy, plays a vital role in sustaining these essential programs and expanding their reach to those who need them most.

We've compiled a directory of organizations we support below. Click on any organization to view their dedicated profile and find out how you can contribute to their cause!
